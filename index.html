Comparatives & Superlatives

How do we compare things?

Short Words (1 syllable)

  • 1 Add -er to compare 2 things. (Fast ‚Üí Faster)
  • 2 Add -est for the top one! (Fast ‚Üí Fastest)

Long Words (3+ syllables)

  • 1 Use more to compare 2 things. (More beautiful)
  • 2 Use most for the top one! (Most beautiful)

The "-y" Rule

If a word ends in -y, change it to -i and then add -er or -est!

Happy ‚Üí Happier ‚Üí Happiest
